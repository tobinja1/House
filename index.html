how to play:

this is a record button. press it to record audio.

press it, and it turns red. this means you’re recording. press it again to stop recording.

this is a play button. press it to play the audio you just recorded.

you can loop the audio you just recorded.

or randomize its pitch.

and randomly crop the recording, too.

press the feedback button instantly output all sounds coming into the mic.

the Magic House uses four different effects. drag your thumb (or mouse) across the multicolored pad below. individual effects are more pronounced in the corners of the box, while they blend together towards the middle.

the Magic House also has two modes: recs mode and samples mode. in samples mode, you use pre-recorded samples instead of your own recordings.
